About Us

LFSB University was introduced on February 20, 2000 in honor of our late former president Linden Forbes Sampson Burnham, produced thousands of graduates not only from students locally but abroad as well.The core objective of LFSB University is to make education accessible and affordable to students across the nation and to students abroad. Students can choose to enhance their knowledge in convenient learning modes without compromising on their jobs and time for family

Our cirriculum is aligned and continuously upgraded to meet the growing industry needs and to empower our students to chart their own future in the demanding professional landscape. Student-focused pedagogy, compassionate and knowledgeable faculty, intellectual diversity, academic rigor, internships, volunteer programs etc, are part of students' life at LFSB University. With a passion for excellence and innovation, Linden Forbes Sampson Burnham is committed on it's mission for making a positive difference in the communities by nurturing thought leaders. When our students succeed, societies benefit and countries prosper at large. Together, let us strive to transform our world into a better place.

Mission Statement:

  • To make students of Guyana and across the globe proud of being part of LFSB University by providing the highest level of education and user friendly faculties.
  • To revolutionize education by offering updated, modern and excellent training in areas of social science, natural science, health science, technology and agriculture that students seek to engage with LFSB university.
Vision Statement:
  • To be the leading university in Guyana by providing the highest level of education, research and innovation.

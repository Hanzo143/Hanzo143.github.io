Faculty of Social Sciences(Details)

The academic programmes offered in the faculty of social sciences at the undergraduate level are:

  1. Bachelor of social science (social work) - The programme has been developed in the close collaboration with partner social work agencies to ensure you develop the skills you need to excel in this challenging profession. (3 years duration)
  2. Requirements: A minimum of five CSEC subjects General proficiency grades I, II, III. Subjects must include English Language

  1. Bachelor of Social Science (Sociology) - The study of the development, structure and functioning of human sociey.(duration: 3 years)
  2. Requirements: A minimum of five CSEC subjects General proficiency grades I, II, III. Subjects must include English Language
  1. Bachelor of Social Science (communication studies) - The study of communication in relationships. These relationships can range anywhere from interpersonal to organizational. (duration: 3 years)
  2. Requirements: A minimum of five CSEC subjects General proficiency grades I, II, III. Subjects must include English Language and Mathematics
  1. Bachelor of Social Science (Public Management) - The course provides graduates with current and future managerial and development skills for the publi service, at local, provincial and national levels of government. (duration: 3 years)
  2. Requirements: A minimum of five CSEC subjects General proficiency grades I, II, III. Subjects must include English Language and Mathematics
  1. Bachelor of Social Science (Economics) - Students learn everything from what determines the price of goods and services to why the average standards of living vary so widely within between countries (duration: 4 years)
  2. Requirements: A minimum of five CSEC subjects General proficiency grades I, II, III. Subjects must include English Language and Mathematics and any business related subject / Diploma in Economics with a pass certificate in Business from GTI (Government Technical Institute)
  1. Bachelor of Social Science (International Relations) - is interdisciplinary in nature, blending the fields of economics, history, and political science to examine topics such as human rights, global poverty, the environment, economics, globalization, security, global ethics and the political environment (duration: 3 years)
  2. Requirements: A minimum of five CSEC subjects General proficiency grades I, II, III. Subjects must include English Language and Mathematics
  1. Bachelor of Law - Teaches you to understand and critically analyze the rules and institutions which society establishes to secure and promote justice and order (duration: 4 years)
  2. Requirements: A minimum of five CSEC subjects General proficiency grades I, II, III. Subjects must include English Language (min. grade II), mathematics and one year pre law
